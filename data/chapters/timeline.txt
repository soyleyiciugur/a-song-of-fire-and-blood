16th of 4th Moon (Day 1): Dragon eggs hatch. King is poisoned at the feast. Lockdown begins. Jace interrogates Rhaella.

17th of 4th Moon (Day 2): King Baelenys drags himself from his deathbed to the Iron Throne. He orders the kitchen staff executed.

18th–19th of 4th Moon (Days 3 & 4): Gaelor tortures the remaining cooks and gets the confession ("The Hand"). Alester finds Rhaella's hidden letter.

20th of 4th Moon (Day 5): [Chapter II begins] Gaelor sets the trap with the false prisoner. The disastrous family feast happens. Visenor is stripped of Blackfyre. That night, Visenor, Rhaella, and Alester flee the city.

The Aftermath & The Wedding (Late 4th to Early 5th Moon)

Late 4th Moon / Early 5th Moon (Chapter III): The royal family travels to Driftmark for Gaelor’s wedding. Jace verifies the Braavosi bank records, confirms Derrin Hightower’s treason, and quietly assassinates the Hand. King Baelenys hires Curtass Whent to track down Visenor.

The Long Road & The Massacre (Mid 5th to 6th Moon)

Mid 5th Moon (Chapter IV & V): Visenor, Rhaella, and Alester are ambushed by Whent’s men in the woods. Alester fights them off. They arrive at Starfall. Alester leaves immediately with Lady Lorenah Dayne's envoy to travel back to King's Landing.

Late 5th Moon: Saera flies North to Winterfell. Gaelor departs King's Landing, marching an army of 2,000 men toward Oldtown (a massive logistical march that takes several weeks).

The 6th Moon (Chapter V & VI):

In Dorne: Visenor and Rhaella spend this month hidden at the Dornish sanctuary. Rhaella discovers she is pregnant. Visenor reads Baelor's letters and learns of Boneskin.

In the Reach: Gaelor arrives in Oldtown. The negotiations fail, resulting in the Oldtown Massacre.

In King's Landing: Great-uncle Baelor arrives. The court stews in tension. Jace trains with the sword. The King and Queen's relationship shatters.

The Dragon's Ascent (7th Moon)

3rd of 7th Moon (Chapter VI & VII): Lady Lorenah Dayne and a battered Ser Alester finally arrive in King's Landing (a 4-to-5 week journey from Dorne). Jace and Lorenah visit Flea Bottom. Jace is knighted by Alester. On this exact day in Dorne, Visenor leaves the sanctuary and begins climbing the Red Mountains.

3rd–5th of 7th Moon: Visenor climbs to the Red Mountains. 
6th–7th of 7th Moon: Visenor finds and waits outside Boneskin's cave, sings to the dragon, and finally "tames" Boneskin.

8th of 7th Moon: Visenor returns to Rhaella.
